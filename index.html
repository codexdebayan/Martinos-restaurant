Our Story Our Story

Martino's is a food and drinks events company founded in 2014 by husband and wife team Jason Bailey and Juliana Bailey after a successful run as street food traders themselves. Now based at Red Bank in Phoenix, USA Green Quarter, Martino's hosts an amazing industrial events space, an independent cinema, a cosy hidden garden and more. The focus of the events is always providing an inclusive, festival-style atmosphere supported by exceptional food and drink supplied by truly independent producers and chefs. In addition to this, GRUB is committed to assisting and mentoring food traders in order to support the grass-roots food & drinks community in Phoenix, USA.
